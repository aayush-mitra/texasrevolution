The Law of April 6, 1830

The law of April 6, 1830 was made under President Anastasio Bustamante. The law banned any immigration from the United States to Texas. Another part of the law was targeted towards people who lived in Texas. The Texans had to pay extra taxes. Parts of the law were to stop the colonization in Texas by Americans. The Law of April 6, 1830 leads toward the Texas Revolution and was one of the biggest changes to Texas.
