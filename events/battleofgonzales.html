The Battle of Gonzales

The battle of Gonzalez started on October 2, 1835. During the Battle of Gonzalez, the Texans refused to give up their cannon to Mexico. The battle ended on the same day, and the Mexicans failed to achieve their goal of obtaining the cannon, but they failed. The Texans won. The Battle of Gonzalez was an important battle ,for it was the first battle. It is known as the "Lexington of Texas."
